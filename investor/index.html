CYG-THESIS-001 / Public memo

The next institutional edge is not more data. It is governed autonomous cognition.

Cygnus X-1 starts from a specific view of markets: price is the temporary clearing level created by constrained participants, fragmented venues, leverage, latency, regulation, and narrative pressure. The investment problem is not simply prediction. It is state inference under constraint.

An AI-native fund should not merely summarize research or accelerate analysts. It should become the native decision architecture for signal ingestion, hypothesis generation, strategy testing, risk veto, execution routing, attribution, and continuous learning.
